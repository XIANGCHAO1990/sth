Usage:

	 

- Press the + button to set the desired temperature (press and hold + - to move up and down quickly) Set the temperature of the display. 

- Press SET to confirm the completion of return, then the controller automatically by setting the relay off! The Thermostat output for the 20A relay to meet a variety of high-power load, connect the control circuit, you can supply power to the thermostat, this display shows the ambient temperature

- Indicator: Flashing indicates the cooling or heating start delay. If the indicator is on, it indicates that the relay is closed

- Digital control: display LL sensor is open, please follow the instructions to connect the sensor; display HH is outside the measuring range will force the relay to disconnect the thermostat; display high temperature alarm.
