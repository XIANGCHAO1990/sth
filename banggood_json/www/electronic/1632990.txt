Features: 
	 

No mixing of materials and no baking. Just knead, flatten and create the impression!

This kit includes soft, pliable air-drying clay.

The non-toxic clay is safe and easy to use to capture those little hands and feet

After you make the impressions, the clay dries into a stone-like material in a couple of days

	 

Specifications:
	 

Material: Pine+Clay

Overall dimensions: 316x208x20mm

Photo Frame Colour: White

Clay Colour: Green; Pink; Blue

Holds one 5 x 7 inch photo

Clay display areas are 4.6 x 6.5 inches

	 

Notice: 
	 

Recommended Ages: 0-3years

	 

Clay Tips: 

	To ensure a smooth dried finish, be sure the clay is rolled out evenly and is as smooth on the surface as possible before making the print. Then, cover the clay with a slightly damp light-weight cloth for 24 hours, or until the clay surface becomes leathery hard. 

Also, the clay is held into the frame by being pressed into the edges of the wooden openings.
