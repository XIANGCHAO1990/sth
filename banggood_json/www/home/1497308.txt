Description:
Dream color strip lights are very distinct from cheap traditional RGB strip lights. With the built-in IC on the strip, these lights can change colors like a rainbow on different parts, which create an ambience like you were in an unrealistic dream. Literally, they are the most ideal substitutes or upgraded version for the outdated RGB strips.
 
Feature:
Individually addressable LED: 
Dream Color USB LED strip light for PC Screen Backlight lighting, play games, watch movies or daily working. It adds ambient backlight effects to computer or laptop compliment on-screen video content. This kit dynamically adjusts elements such as color and brightness to create a more immersive multimedia experience. By the way, comfortable bias lighting will reduce eye fatigue between the bright PC and dark room.

Dream color: 
Each LED is individually addressable. Each LED can be independently discolored according to the display that will give you a better visual effect and a better viewing experience. LED Strip will do work after soft installation, you must know that it won't work if you use it for your normal TV rather than Windows.

AMBIBOX-The lighting features: 
Color balance setting for each LED strip separately. Individual adjustable capture area for each LED strip. Overall average color for all areas calculation mode. Permanent backlight (lamp) mode. Capture from video. Capture from games (GDI, DirectX 10/11). Support max 300LEDs.
This item can be used in anywhere conveniently and get perfect work in summer, create a fantastic camping experience for you.
A perfect decoration for Christmas, Party, Wedding, New Year.
