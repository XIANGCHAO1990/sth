Features:

It is a mini 200A True RMS auto range digital clamp meter with 2000 display count portable and multifunctional device.


Voltage and current RMS measurement 

With high-precision frequency measurement function.Minimum differentiate 0.001HZ

With up to differentiate 1pF maximum measured capacitance auto range measuring function of 2000uf;

With no-contact AC voltage sensing measurements(NVC) function;
		Auto range,2-3/s sampling rate

Have a measurable voltage drop of 3V diode measurements.Very convenient LED judge good or bad;

Data Hold function,automatic shutdown and backlight;

Temperature measurement,°C and °F for choice,small and easy to carry.
