Description:

	This digital meter which can display temperature and humidity simultaneously, with maximum and minimum value display. It is suitable for desktop placing or wall hanging.

	 

Features:
	 

Measure temperature and humidity at the same time.

Touch key to switch temperature unit: Celsius(°C) or Fahrenheit(°F).

Maximum & minimum value, temperature and humidity trend, comfort level display.

2 magnetic blocks, can be easily attached to some metal materials.

With a hanging hole, can be hung on the wall.

With a bracket, it can be placed on the table.

Powered by 2 1.5V AAA batteries(not included).
