LED Light Water Tap Temperature Sensor RGB Glow Shower Stream  3 Color Changing or 7 Color Flashing Bathroom Faucet Showerhead
 

- No batteries and external power supply, it will illuminate after water flows down.

- When the water source is turned off, the light will go off immediately.

- Transforming the stream of water into a beautiful waterfall of light.

- Ideal for bathroom or kitchen to make it fantastic. 

- Offer you a delight & wonderful experience.

	 

3 Colors Temperature Control and 7 Colors Flashing Faucet Showerhead for your choice ( First is 3 Colors LED Temperature Control, second is 7 Colors Flashing Showerhead )

	 

The color of the faucet's light can change one color according to time every 1-2s.

The several color light at random, forming a colorful change effect.

At the same time, which makes the light quite beautiful.

	 

LED Temperature Control Shower Head :

	 

The sensitive faucet light will change colors according to the temperature of water.

The color‘s change remind you the water's temperature.

	 

Display blue when the water temperature is below 32°.

Display green when the water temperature is between 33-41°.

Displays red when the water temperature is between 42-45°.

Water flash red when temperature above 46°C.
