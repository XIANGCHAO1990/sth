Description:

	 

The Magnetic Base has permanent magnet inside the metal block body, with the "on/off" or "+ / -"switch to open or shade the magnetic field to hold on the ferrous metal surface or remove the magnetic base. The magnetic base has the screw hole on the top surface to install holderfor dial indicator, gauge, line cutting arm, lifting bar and etc.

	 

Note: 1,The Magnetic holding force is depend on the holding ferrous material, thickness, flatness, roughness and etc.

	 

          2, 30kg is the type tested max holding break force, not the bending or friction force.

	 

          3, Because of the interior magnet is made of strong Ndfeb material, the rotating support is special, so operate the switch
